The fishery: now versus later Slides 31–37

The DP skeleton (slides 35–36): decision 0 ≤ xₜ ≤ bₜ, reward r(xₜ) − c(xₜ, bₜ), state transition bₜ₊₁ = 1.2(bₜ − xₜ), recursion fₜ(bₜ) = max { r − c + fₜ₊₁(1.2(bₜ − xₜ)) }.
spoiler — open after the class has answeredWith pure revenue and no catching costs the recursion says: wait and take everything in year 5 (20 736). Real r and c bend that answer — but the state equation is what carries “the future” into today’s decision.
